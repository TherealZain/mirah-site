Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Mirah (“we”, “us”, the “App”) is an AI-powered digital closet and outfit planner for Muslim and modest-fashion women. This policy explains what we collect, why, how it is stored, and the choices you have. We collect only what the App needs to work. We do not sell your data, we do not show ads, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

This App is operated by Zain Zulfiqar, United States. Questions: zzainzulfiqar@gmail.com.

We do not collect: contacts, browsing history, advertising identifiers, health data, or any data for advertising or cross-app tracking. The App contains no advertising SDKs; its only analytics is the anonymized, non-tracking service described above.

How your data is used by AI

Your garment photos and preferences are sent to our secure backend, which calls AI model providers (currently Google’s Gemini) to detect garments and generate outfit suggestions. These providers process the data to return a result and, per their enterprise terms, do not use it to train their models. Your photos are never made public; closet images live in a private, per-user storage bucket accessible only with your authenticated session.

How it is stored and protected

Data is stored with our infrastructure provider, Supabase (Postgres database and private file storage). Every user-owned record is protected by row-level security so one account can never read another’s data. Payments are handled by Apple; subscription state is mirrored via RevenueCat. We retain your data for as long as your account exists.

Your choices and rights

  • Edit or delete individual items any time from your closet.
  • Delete your account from Profile → Delete Account. This permanently and irreversibly erases your profile, closet, photos, outfit history, and remaining credits from our systems.
  • Turn off weather/location by declining the location permission or not using Pro weather styling — the App still works.
  • Access or export your data, or ask questions, by emailing zzainzulfiqar@gmail.com.

If you are in a region with specific privacy rights (e.g. GDPR/UK GDPR, CCPA), you may request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your data, and you may lodge a complaint with your local authority. We honor these requests at zzainzulfiqar@gmail.com.

Children

Mirah is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, an in-app notice.
