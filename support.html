Frequently asked

How do I add clothes to my closet?

Tap the + on the Closet tab and take a photo (or pick one from your library). Mirah’s AI detects the garment and fills in the details — you just confirm and save.

How do I get an outfit suggestion?

Open the Stylist tab, pick an occasion (work, casual, a cultural event), and tap Generate. Mirah builds outfits from pieces you already own, respecting your coverage and modesty preferences. Tap Customize to swap any piece.

What do subscriptions and Enhance credits unlock?

Free includes your closet and core modest styling. Starter and Pro raise your wardrobe and outfit limits and add weather-aware styling. Enhance credits power AI photo cleanup of individual garments. Manage or cancel anytime in the App Store under your Apple account → Subscriptions.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Open the paywall (Profile → upgrade, or any upgrade prompt) and tap Restore Purchases.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Profile → Delete Account. This permanently and irreversibly erases your profile, closet, photos, outfit history, and remaining credits.

Do you sell my data or show ads?

No. Mirah has no ads and never sells your data. See our Privacy Policy.
